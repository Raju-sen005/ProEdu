Awareness Programs on Water & Sanitation (Jal Jeevan Mission)

As part of our commitment to societal well-being, ProEdu Skills conducts awareness programs on water and sanitation in rural communities under the Jal Jeevan Mission. These programs educate participants on the importance of clean water, hygiene, and sanitation practices. By empowering individuals with the knowledge to improve public health and environmental sustainability, we contribute to the broader goal of ensuring access to safe drinking water for all.

Workshops for Career Growth

Our career-focused workshops are tailored to equip students with the practical skills and knowledge necessary for thriving in today’s competitive job market. These workshops are designed to enhance employability by offering training in various job roles aligned with theSkill India Mission. Through interactive sessions, expert guidance, and hands-on training, we aim to help students unlock their full potential and pursue meaningful career paths.
